About Story Formats

A story format controls the behavior, and to a degree, the appearance of your story once it's built and viewed in a Web browser. Each story format's fonts and colors can be customized for your story.

Sugarcane
Each passage is displayed one at a time to the reader, and certain passages can be rewound to through a menu.

Jonah
All passages are displayed on a single page that expands as the reader clicks links.

TiddlyWiki 2 and TiddlyWiki 1
These formats allow you to publish to the format that Jonah and Sugarcane are based on. They are general-purpose wikis that can be easily browsed and modified.
